# FastTrack Support                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                           
  ## About FastTrack                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                           
  FastTrack is a precision acceleration timer for iOS. Track your 0-60 mph, quarter-mile times, and full drives using GPS
  technology. Share your runs and compete with friends.                                                                    
                                                                                                                           
  ## Features                                                                                                              
   
  - 0-60 mph and 0-100 mph timing                                                                                          
  - Quarter-mile and half-mile runs
  - Full drive tracking with route maps
  - Vehicle garage management
  - Social sharing and leaderboards

  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  **Q: Why does FastTrack need location access?**
  A: FastTrack uses GPS to measure your speed and distance with precision. Location data is only collected during active
  timing sessions.

  **Q: Is my data private?**
  A: Your run data is stored securely. Shared runs are only visible to friends you add.

  **Q: The app shows poor GPS accuracy. What should I do?**
  A: Ensure you're outdoors with a clear view of the sky. GPS accuracy improves after a few seconds of signal acquisition.
